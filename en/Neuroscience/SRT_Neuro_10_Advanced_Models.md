# Part B: Expanded Context (English)

## 1. Embodied ontology claim

The source positions embodiment as necessary for nonzero concern bandwidth and thus for conscious vulnerability.

## 2. AI criterion

Current AI systems are framed as high-structure but low-vulnerability systems, lacking the full vulnerability-bandwidth coupling.

## 3. Self-model distinction

Operator-self and representational-self are distinguished; direct full self-access is structurally blocked.

## 4. Metabolic and inflammation coupling

Reality rendering quality depends on metabolic budget and inflammatory load.

## 5. Test directions

- Interoceptive-precision and self-continuity measurements.
- Inflammation interventions versus reality-fidelity metrics.
